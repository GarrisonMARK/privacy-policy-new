Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

a) Information You Voluntarily Provide

Currently, playing Match Chaos does not require you to provide any direct personal information.

In the future, we plan to offer an option to sign up for testing our upcoming games. If you choose to sign up, we will ask you to provide the following information so we can contact you:

  • Your email address.
  • Your name or a chosen username (optional).

Providing this information is completely voluntary and is not required to play Match Chaos. You would only provide it if you wish to be contacted about future testing opportunities.

b) Information Collected Automatically by Third Parties

Even if you do not provide information to us directly, our third-party partners (specifically Google) automatically collect certain information when you play our game. This is necessary to deliver advertisements that support the game and to analyze our game's performance. This information includes:

  • Device and Usage Information: Your IP address, device type, unique device identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID), operating system, and general location (e.g., country or city).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Serve Advertisements: The data collected automatically by our partner, Google, is used to provide personalized ads within the game, which keeps the game free to play.
  • To Contact You for Game Testing: If you voluntarily sign up for our game testing program, we will use the email address you provide to send you invitations and information related to testing our future games.
  • To Maintain and Improve Our Services: We use the automatically collected data to understand and analyze trends, to improve the game, and to ensure our advertising services are working correctly.
